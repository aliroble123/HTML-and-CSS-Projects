Classic Cheeseburger

Ingredients

  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 beef patty
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Ketchup

Directions

  1. Cook the patty.
  2. Add cheese.
  3. Toast the bun.
  4. Assemble the burger.
  5. Serve hot.
